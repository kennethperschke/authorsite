VOIDWALKER

A novel by Kenneth Perschke

A chilling journey into a world where the sky bleeds red and reality is a broken, distorted song. Daniel and his silent brother Micah must confront a force that seeks to name and unravel them, before they lose themselves entirely to the void.

About the Author

Kenneth Perschke is a writer of literary horror and speculative fiction, known for crafting worlds that are both beautiful and deeply unsettling. His work is inspired by the surreal, dreamlike narratives of authors like Neil Gaiman, exploring themes of identity, memory, and the unseen forces that shape reality.

When not writing, he can be found exploring nature or reading old books.
